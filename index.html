The Tale of Mia and Leo

One day, Mia received a bunch of messages. Some said things like “You did a great job! 🎉” and others said “This is not good.” Mia asked Leo: “How can I sort these quickly?”

Leo smiled, “We can look for clues!”

  • Positive words like great, happy, awesome
  • Negative words like bad, angry, terrible
  • Negation words like not, never flip the feeling
  • Emojis and punctuation like ! can make feelings stronger

“So we add up the clues,” said Leo. “If the total is high → positive, if it’s low → negative, and if it’s around zero → neutral.”
